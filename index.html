Our Story

When I was in high school I was interested in my computer and art classes, I certainly wasn’t looking for love. That was until I realized how funny this girl I didn’t know thought I was! We had a few classes together senior year (and turns out every other year, and never knew it!) so we started talking. Soon I found myself changing where I sat to sit closer to her and I didn’t even mind getting woken up in class to receive notes from her!

I took the chance and asked her to go to Prom with me and she said yes! We’ve shared many life experiences together and have grown a lot since then. Now I can’t wait to spend the rest of my life with her and call Emily my wife!

- Cameron's Story

Way back in 2008, during the last quarter of senior year, I decided to sit at a table in marketing with two of my friends and a kid I’d never met. Little did I know, that kid would not only eventually become my date to senior prom, but my boyfriend, and now my future husband!

Over the past six years, Cameron and I have been through many of life’s great adventures. We’ve seen each other through college, a few job changes, even rescuing our cat, Bolin, from the grocery store where we worked! The past several years have been an amazing ride, and now I can’t wait to see what fun the rest of our lives have to offer!

- Emily's Story

Groomsmen

  • Chris’s story starts off like so many other brotherly stories, He annoyed me, He followed me, he tried to take my things, but now he’s like a little me and we’re best friends with similar interests in computers and programming. My wedding wouldn’t be complete without Chris as my Best man.

  • Randy was one of the first friends I met in the beginning of high school. Our friendship survived all four years of school shifting in and out of friend groups together making Randy on of my oldest friends. If anyone has any dirt on me, it’s definitely this guy which why he shares the responsibility of Best man!

  • Roy and I met while I worked at Wegmans, we both we’re on helping hands working outside in the parking lot gave us lots of time to get to know each other. Roy is truly one of the first friends of mine that became equally good friends with Emily making him our friend that we still hang out with long after Emily and I have left working with him which is why I’m honored to call him a Groomsmen.

  • Graham, I also met during my tenure at Wegmans. I remember working with Graham would make those long shifts fly by, and I always liked how bothered he would get when he had to endure a full 8 hour shift while I came in for a brief 4 hours. We had loads of topics in common for us to converse about during those days and today which is why he’s one my Groomsmen.

Bridesmaids

  • I like to think Sandra remembers the day I was born as the best day of her life, mainly because it was the day I made her a big sister! Sandra and I have been through it all together, and I can honestly say that God gave me the best sister in the world. Whether we’re dancing in the car or singing off key to some ridiculous song, we’re always laughing. Sandra’s my right hand gal, and I couldn’t imagine a better maid of honor!

  • A long time ago, when I was nine, I met Rae. We bonded over our love for Mary-Kate and Ashley movies, Leonardo DiCaprio, and lip gloss. We’ve done everything together, from being in the same Girl Scout troop to working at Caribou Coffee, and at this point, Rae’s basically my second sister. I’m excited to not only call her my oldest friend, but my maid of honor as well!

  • Michelle and I met during our first year of middle school. There were a lot of laughs over silly things and funky hair accessories that year! Now that we’re older, we spend most of our time talking about comics and obsessing over The Avengers and all things Marvel. Michelle is probably the sweetest person I know, and I couldn’t imagine getting married without her as my bridesmaid!

  • Jessica and I met during P.E. in 8th grade, and we’ve pretty much been best friends since. She’s my concert buddy, my tattoo buddy, my crazy cat lady buddy, and I love her for all of it! Jessica’s probably the most artistically talented person I’ve ever met (she made her own prom dress!), and I am more than honored to not only call her my florist, but also my bridesmaid!
