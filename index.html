Our Story

When I was in high school I was interested in my computer and art classes, I certainly wasn’t looking for love. That was until I realized how funny this girl I didn’t know thought I was! We had a few classes together senior year (and turns out every other year, and never knew it!) so we started talking. Soon I found myself changing where I sat to sit closer to her and I didn’t even mind getting woken up in class to receive notes from her!

I took the chance and asked her to go to Prom with me and she said yes! We’ve shared many life experiences together and have grown a lot since then. Now I can’t wait to spend the rest of my life with her and call Emily my wife!

- Cameron's Story

Way back in 2008, during the last quarter of senior year, I decided to sit at a table in marketing with two of my friends and a kid I’d never met. Little did I know, that kid would not only eventually become my date to senior prom, but my boyfriend, and now my future husband!

Over the past six years, Cameron and I have been through many of life’s great adventures. We’ve seen each other through college, a few job changes, even rescuing our cat, Bolin, from the grocery store where we worked! The past several years have been an amazing ride, and now I can’t wait to see what fun the rest of our lives have to offer!

- Emily's Story
